## Corporate Profile & Mission

**Aero Nova Technologies** is establishing a high-efficiency ultra-fast EV charging infrastructure tailored for high-performance sedans, extended-range SUVs, and heavy-duty utility trucks. By blending structural aesthetics with hyper-engineered delivery, we aim to provide instant luxury EV replenishment.

### Key Infrastructure Highlights
* **350kW Power Output:** Ultra-fast liquid-cooled loops delivering rapid energy replenishment.
* **Dual Compatibility:** Hardware integrations supporting both **NACS** and **CCS V2** standard connectors.
* **Optimized Pricing Infrastructure:** Structured baseline charging rates mapped uniformly at **$0.42 / kWh**.
* **Constant System Monitoring:** 24/7 central command monitoring dispatch to track hardware status, grid parameters, and billing authorizations.
